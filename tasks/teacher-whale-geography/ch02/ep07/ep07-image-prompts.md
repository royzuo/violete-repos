# Nano Banana 图片提示词

## 1. 环礁形成示意图
Prompt: Create a clean educational diagram of atoll formation: volcanic island subsiding, coral reefs growing upward, central lagoon forming, with simple Chinese labels, flat infographic style, blue ocean palette, high clarity.

## 2. 珊瑚白化对比图
Prompt: Create a scientific comparison illustration showing healthy coral reef versus bleached coral reef, split-screen layout, labeled in simplified Chinese, explanatory style, underwater light, high educational clarity.

## 3. 海平面上升与海岸侵蚀图
Prompt: Create a cross-section diagram of a low-lying coral island under sea level rise, showing groundwater salinization, beach erosion, homes, roads, seawall, and wave action, simplified Chinese labels, textbook infographic style.
